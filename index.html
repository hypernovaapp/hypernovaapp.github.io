Privacy Policy

This mobile application respects and values your privacy. Below are the privacy policies related to the use of the application:

Collection of Personal Information

This application does not collect any type of personal information from the user, such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.

Task Lists Data

The application only works with task lists created by the user and stores them locally on their mobile device. This data is kept private and is not shared with third parties.

Security

No specific security measures are implemented, as the user's task lists are stored locally on their mobile device. It's important to note that no data transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. Therefore, it is recommended to take additional precautions to protect the information on your device.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is subject to changes and updates. Important changes will be notified through the application or via another suitable medium.
